Discover the Joy of Swimming At Little Splashes Aquatics 

At Little Splashes Aquatics, we nurture children’s love for water through child-centered, holistic swim programs that emphasize fun, safety, and skill-building.

Intro with Image

Little Splashes Aquatics‘ first main aim is to boost a child’s water confidence and respect for the water, where these behaviours are highly important in teaching water safety. Water safety is consistently the main priority in all Little Splashes’ swim programs. Little Splashes was developed based on more than 20 years of teaching experience, passion, and background in early childhood psychology, to cater to every age group and according to the developmental milestones of each child. Our child-centred curriculum has been carefully put together to ensure holistic learning, maximum participation time, constant engagement and social interaction. Each water activity and every routine practice of a targeted skill through play and songs, serves a purpose in our vision. This is why many customers recognise us as one of the best swim school in Singapore.

Features

Child-Centric Approach and Facilities

At Little Splashes, we prioritize your child's happiness and engagement in every lesson. Our classes feature a variety of fun water toys and interactive songs that transform swimming into an exciting adventure. We believe in small class sizes, ensuring personalized attention and fostering a comfortable learning environment. Our facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art water quality systems, including advanced Ozone and Mineral Swim systems, ensuring a safe and hygienic space for young swimmers. 

Learn more

Holistic Development

Little Splashes goes beyond teaching swimming; our programs are designed to foster holistic development. We focus on enhancing social interaction, motor skills, and emotional resilience, preparing children for life both in and out of the pool. Through group activities and collaborative games, children learn essential skills like teamwork and communication. We also engage parents by providing resources and tips to support their child’s swimming journey at home, ensuring a well-rounded experience that nurtures confidence and self-esteem.

Learn more

Certified and Caring Coaches

Our dedicated coaches are professionally certified, holding qualifications from recognized organizations such as AUSTSWIM, SSI and STA. With a minimum of 500 hours in house training, coaches are adept and skilled at teaching your child. Our coaches employ effective communication techniques and positive reinforcement strategies to ensure every lesson is both enjoyable and constructive. They are passionate about nurturing your child's swimming skills while promoting confidence and a sense of accomplishment.

Our Progression Structure

A clear, actionable multi stage process for your child to learn swimming from infancy to proficiency
Little Splasher:
Otter Level


For infants aged 3-11 Months, learn water sensory experiences, basic language instruction and social awareness
Little Splasher:
Seal Level


For Pre Toddlers aged 12 to 23 Months, learn  independent water movement and stroke fundamentals
Little Splasher:
Dolphin Level


For Pre Toddlers aged 24 to 36 Months, learn independent swimming strokes, stroke fundamentals and emotional development  
Big Splasher: 
Penguin Level


For Children above age 3 in Basic level of Learn to Swim,  learn water safety and basic swimming skills, focusing on building confidence and independence.
Big Splasher: 
Barracuda Level


For Children above age 3 in Intermediate level of Learn to Swim, learn to stroke development, basic sculling and survival skills. 
Big Splasher:
Orca Level


For Children above age 3 in Advanced level of Learn to Swim, learn personal water survival and stroke developmental skills, in preparation for for SwimSafer 2.0 Advanced Stage
