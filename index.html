Discover the Joy of Swimming At Little Splashes Aquatics 

At Little Splashes Aquatics, we nurture children’s love for water through child-centered, holistic swim programs that emphasize fun, safety, and skill-building.

Who We Are 

At Little Splashes Aquatics, we bring swimming to life through our child-focused methodology. We believe in making lessons fun and interactive, using engaging water toys, songs, and games to help children feel confident and excited in the water. Our milestone-driven syllabus ensures each lesson aligns with your child’s physical, social, and emotional development.

With warm, mineral-heated pools and certified coaches who specialize in positive reinforcement, we focus on building water safety skills, coordination, and teamwork. While our core focus is on children, we also offer adult programs to promote fitness, water safety, and overall well-being, ensuring a holistic experience for all ages.

Features

Child-Centric Approach and Facilities

Our lessons turn swimming into fun through water toys, songs, and learning tools, tailored to each child’s developmental milestones. With heated pools (31-33°C) and a unique Mineral Swim System using Dead Sea minerals and ozone purification, we ensure a comfortable and skin-friendly environment for all ages.

Learn more

Holistic Development

We nurture more than just swimming skills—our programs boost social, motor, and emotional development. Through group activities, children learn teamwork, communication, and resilience, building confidence both in and out of the pool. Parents receive resources and tips to support their child’s progress at home.

Learn more

Certified and Caring Coaches

Our certified coaches (AUSTSWIM, SSI, STA) undergo 500+ hours of in-house training. They combine positive reinforcement with expert teaching to build swimming skills, confidence, and self-esteem, creating a supportive and enjoyable learning experience for every child.

Our Indoor Heated Swimming Pool Locations 

At Little Splashes, all of our swimming lessons take place in our signature indoor mineral-heated pools. We maintain water temperatures between 31-33°C for the perfect learning environment. Our Mineral Swim System delivers crystal-clear water that’s gentle on skin, making it ideal for babies, toddlers, and even those with sensitive skin conditions like eczema.
